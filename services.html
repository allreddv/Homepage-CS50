CS50's Services

Using our patented algorithims we are able to provide your best friend an individually tailored walk like no other.

  1. Fire Hidrant Optimization

    Our algorithm will calculate a walking route that maximizes the number of fire hidrants to pass within your walking distance.

  2. Doggie Face Recognition

    Using your cell phone camera and our App, you can scan other dogs on the walk and it will show name, sex, and agressivness for each dog, so you know which dogs you can approach.

    Never lose a buried bone again. Our geo-location mapping will remeber every buried bone and will beep when nearing a prized treasure.
